Meet the Doctor

15 Years of Healing with Purpose

Dr. Priya Sharma completed her MBBS from Osmania Medical College and MD (General Medicine) from Nizam's Institute of Medical Sciences. She founded Sharma Family Clinic in 2009 with one belief: quality healthcare should be accessible to everyone.

Fluent in Telugu, Hindi, and English. She takes time to truly listen to every patient, explaining diagnoses clearly and involving families in care decisions.
